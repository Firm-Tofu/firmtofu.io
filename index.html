Our Services

We offer a variety of services to help your vegan business succeed.

Branding

As your brand is the first thing your customers will see, creatiing a memorable impression is key. At Firm Tofu, we specialize in developing well-thought out and impactful logos and branding to help vegan companies and organizations stand out among the rest.

Graphic Design

Your business's success hinges upon how well you communicate your business and your mission. We offer graphic design solutions for all your project and business needs. From vegan products packaging, event posters and brochures to stunning mobile responsive websites to showcase your business, inspire your current audience and increase your visibility to potential customers.

Financial Solutions

We have strategic partnerships which allow us to offer deeply discounted financial solutions, including tax services, alternative funding and business planning.
